Why We Collect Data on Student Progress

At GIC, we are committed to providing the best educational experience for our students. Collecting data on student progress allows us to:

  • Identify areas where students excel and where they need additional support.
  • Tailor academic programs to better meet the needs of our students.
  • Improve overall student satisfaction and success rates.
  • Make informed decisions about curriculum development and resource allocation.

Your participation in this data collection effort is crucial in helping us achieve these goals.
